Introduction

In TotPlayer you can create various playsets for your child by selecting songs (sounds) from your media library or by recording them using your iphone or ipad.

TotPlayer comes with one pre-loaded playset that has 6 traditional children's songs (source: freekidsmusic.com)

Adding a song to TotPlayer

  • Tap Edit to switch to Edit Mode.

  • Tap Add to open new item screen.

  • On new item screen you can either select a song from your media library or record it using your device's recorder.

  • You also need to select one or more images from your photo library to be displayed while the song is playing. If you select several images they will be displayed as slideshow.

  • Tap Save to save your new item.

Recording your content

Your tot would love to hear your voice!

  • Pre-download some images that will accompany your recording.

  • Tap Edit to switch to Edit Mode -> Add and tap Record to record a word, song, poem or even a short story.

  • Select one or more images to be displayed while the sound is playing.

  • Tap Save to save your new item.

Deleting a song

  • Tap Edit to switch to Edit Mode.

  • Long tap on the song you'd like to delete'.

  • Tap Delete button (appears in the center of navigation bar).
